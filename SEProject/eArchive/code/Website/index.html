How do we monitor health through sports data?

Our results are two ratios, intension to involvement and involvement of each sports.

We can use this statistics to give a index of health judged by the ratio. Take BaketBall for example, according to related studies, people consume 350k calorie per hour, , according to statistics, regular BasketBall game lasts two hours, thus consuming 700k calorie.

Now we have the consumed calorie, we can calculate how many energy have been burnt during one involvement. In our study, we only concern relations between health condition and energy burnt by doing sports. For authorized study, click here.
