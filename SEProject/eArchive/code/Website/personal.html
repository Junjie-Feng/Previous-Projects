Personal Exercise Information

Our Health Monitor can allow user to update their data through their personal account in this website or we can use API to get exercise data mainly from twitter.

Our system has two functions. One is the ratio of each sports, indicating the ratio between people talk about and actually do it.

The other is ratio between each kinds of sports, we can use this raio to see which kind of aport is more popular in our statistics.

Our results are based on our data derived from twitter and user updates, may be not accurate.

Relations with health

All our data are about sports, so what's it relation with health?

As we all know, health is highly related to exercising, such as sports. First, according to United Nations study, effective exercise for 2 hours everyday can reduce 98% of regular dieases. So we can say that people involved into more kinds and time are healthier than people do not (Without considering other factors).

Second, we calculate average calorie each sport consume, assume each person do one sport for a certain period of time, thus calculate the total consumed calorie, cpmpared to the intake calorie, monitoring health condition through monitoring calorie.

Third, after data claculate and graph made, we can show our results on the information page, which displays all the information: Ratio of involvement, Ratio between sports, Health sports (By average consumed calorie) and health persons. Also, there will be links to papers and websites towards a healthier lifestyle.

Some of our results are made by NTLK language and calculated according to average data, so it may be not accurate, but we can still use these results as a reference.
